Postscripts

All the fools' talents end up selling Frozen hearts to the ghosts befriending the winter souls That howl to the westering night but hear not the call Of the warm-spell arriving at the war-horn's sounding.

That's the right word of Thursday's doom. Renegade elves run now behind The gleam of fire refracted in gloomy halls between The finest scheduled lines scored by the tide-lord.

Talented elf-lords always buy the brightest Banners to mark the times until the closing of the night And melting of the frost-right. Clearly the spring is running. On Saturday the toll Sits shrouded for tomorrow.
